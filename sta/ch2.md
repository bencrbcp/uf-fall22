## Sample space

Sample space: Set of all possible outcomes of a statistical experiment, represented by _S_.

## Events

Event: A subset of a sample space

Intersection: Intersection of A and B, denoted A ∩ B, is the event containing all elements common to A and B

Mutually Exclusive: Two events are mutually exclusive, or disjoint if A ∩ B =\= Ø if A and B have no events in common.

The union of two events A and B, denoted A ∪ B is the event containing all the elements that belong to A, B or both
